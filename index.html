HARE KRISHNA

The "Self care-school" program is a transformative initiative led by a group of dedicated and inspired youths whose collective goal is to delve into the pursuit of a higher purpose in life. Embracing the principles of simplicity, self-discovery, and spiritual growth, this program offers participants a unique opportunity to experience the essence of living like a monk. Our program provides an immersive experience that brings together ancient teachings with modern-day living. Through guided workshops, meditation sessions, and reflective exercises, participants learn to cultivate mindfulness, gratitude, and compassion. They are encouraged to let go of material attachments and embrace a simpler, more purposeful way of life.
